What We Do

CarbonCLAIR is a pioneering environmental technology company driven by a mission to enhance urban air quality through innovative solutions. Founded by a team of dedicated engineering students, our flagship product, the CarbonCLAIR Mobile unit, captures particulate matter (PM2.5) and carbon emissions in real-time, using a process that operates efficiently on less than 130 watts. This energy-efficient technology allows for sustainable air purification in diverse settings, from construction sites to residential areas.
